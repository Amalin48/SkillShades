Skin Types

Before applying makeup, it is essential to understand your skin type to choose the right products.

üí° Hover (PC) or tap (mobile) on a skin type to see an example image.

Normal Skin

Well-balanced, healthy moisture and smooth texture.

Dry Skin

Lacks moisture and may feel tight or flaky.

Oily Skin

Excess sebum production with a shiny appearance.

Combination

Oily T-zone with normal or dry cheeks.

Sensitive

Easily irritated and prone to redness.

Acne Prone

Frequent breakouts and clogged pores.
